How to play checkers?

  • The game is based for two players with 12 pieces on each side.
  • All pieces on the board can only move diagonally and forward
  • For your piece to eat the opponents, you need to be diagonal of them and there must be an empty diagonal spot behind them.
  • When your piece reaches the end of the board on the opponents side, it becomes a "king". It has the same movement but can move forward AND backwards.
  • Someone wins when they ate all their opponents pieces or have them locked to where they can't move anywhere.
